Best Cities in the World: Travel Edition

My Top 5 Favorite Cities That I've Been To

  1. Phuket, Thailand
    • Phuket has been the most beautiful and magical place I have ever traveled to. The beaches are unreal and the weather was amazing. I would definitely go back in a heart beat. I spent almost every day on the beach or in a local cafe. Nothing has compared to it!
  2. Tokyo, Japan
    • Tokyo is my second favorite city and I loved every second of it. Although, I also visited Kyoto and Osaka, Tokyo has my heart the most. The culture, the food and the city-life is amazing. I can't wait to go back soon!
  3. San Juan, Puerto Rico
    • Puerto Rico was definitely a vibe and I can spend every night dancing there. The beaches are stunning and the food is delicious. As a latina, I love the music and the people!
  4. Bali, Indonesia
    • Bali was such a serene and peaceful place. I got to cross off something on my bucket list which was yoga in a Bali villa surrounded by jungle. It was one of the coolest things I've ever done. I would love to spend some more months there in the future. I loved exploring the beaches and the rice terraces.
  5. Montego Bay, Jamaica
    • Jamaica was such a fun and lively place to visit. I loved going on adventures like zip-lining and river rafting. I was able to spoil myself a lot on this trip so that was a huge plus lol!

My Top 5 Bucket List Cities to Visit

  • Paris, France
    • I've always dreamed of going to Paris ever since I was a little girl. The fashion, the art- everything about Paris is so appealing to me. I can't wait to visit the Eiffel Tower and see the city lights at night.
  • Providenciales, Turks and Caicos Islands
    • I've heard so many amazing things about Turks and Caicos from friends and I would love to visit one day. The water looks so clear! It just looks like the perfect place to relax and unwind.
  • Male, Maldives
    • The Maldives also looks like a dream! The overwater bungalows is something I want to experience in this lifetime. I would love to go snorkeling and have a perfect vacation there.
  • Sydney, Australia
    • Sydney is actually one of the places I want to study abroad at. It is at the top of my list. I would love to explore the city and go on adventures like hiking and surfing.
  • Barcelona, Spain
    • Spain is such a beautiful country and I would love to visit Barcelona, Madrid, and Seville to name a few. My hope is to travel there in June and July, so I am crossing my fingers! The architecture and culture looks so unique and interesting. I would love to try all the food and explore the city!
